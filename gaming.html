Virtual Reality (VR)

Virtual Reality has come a long way from its early days. With advancements in VR headsets, motion tracking, and haptic feedback, gamers can now immerse themselves in realistic and interactive environments.

Neural interfaces are the next frontier in gaming technology. By directly connecting the brain to the game, players experience an unprecedented level of immersion and control.
